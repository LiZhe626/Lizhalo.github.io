英语复试常见问题

1.Why choose our school?为什么选择我们学校？

​ Thank you for your question. Firstly, I think xxx is a city with a strong academic atmosphere here, which is very suitable for studying. Secondly, I think further education is the first and foremost thing for me to achieve my value. I wish to pursue a higher degree. Studying at your university can help me to meet top students and scholars. Thirdly, I want to do my best to come here, and I hope that I can make contributions to the society with my professional ability. That’s all, Thank you for your time.

2.本科学校

​ Thank you for your question. Firstly, I had studied in xxx university since 2018, which located in xx of xx. I like this university very much. Secondly, it has a beautiful campus and provides us with a lot of chance for academic studies and other activities. Thirdly, My favorite place is the library which has plenty of books. I have to thank my teacher from undergraduate college. His academic and thought infected me and attracted me to the academic road. I’m very grateful to my undergraduate school. That’s all, Thank you for your time.

3.为什么读研？

​ Thank you for your question. Firstly, during the past four years, I have learnt the basic knowledge and relevant skills of my major. However, I realize it is not very enough for my future development. Secondly, if I don’t take the postgraduate entrance examination, I will regret it forever. I think it was the most correct decision for me. This progress itself is very wonderful. Thirdly, I believe further education is the first and foremost thing for me to achieve my value. That’s all, Thank you for your time.

4.未来计划

​ Thank you for your question. If I’m lucky enough to be admited to xxx university, I will devote
all my efforts and energy to my major. Firstly, I will learn the professional knowledge and relevant skills of my major. Secondly, I will improve my academic ability and determine my own
research direction. I hope I can make contributions to the society with my professional ability.
If necessary, I will continue my research and study to earn the higher degree. That’s all, Thank you for your time.

5.hometown 家乡

​ Thank you for your question. My hometown is Tianjin, a beautiful coastal city in northen China. Thats the place where I was born and grew up. The people here are very friendly and welcoming. I love my hometown, including its landscape, special food , culture and so on. If you have the chance to take a walk with me on the streets of Tianjin, you will fall in love with this city. Welcome to the beautiful city. That’s all, Thank you for your time.

6.优点 What’s your greatest strength?

​ Thank you for your question. In my view, my greatest strength is the ability to think independently. But I don’t have this strength by myself. I have to think my teacher Mr xx from
the undergraduate college. His academic and thought infected me and attracted me to the academic road. I gradually have the basic ability of independent thinking. What’s more, I become more and more confident and optimistic. Learning and living are no longer blank and
hopeless for me, but have great direction and hope. That’s all, Thank you for your time.

7.缺点 What’s your greatest weakness?

​ Thank you for your question. In my view, my biggest shortcoming is that I am not good at releasing my pressure, which is due to my high requirements for myself, I no longer have free time in my life, so sometimes I feel tired physically and mentally. Last month, I read some books to learn some skills to make a better balance between my studies and life. I believe that I will make progress and over come my shortcomings int the future. That’s all, Thank you for your time.

8.家庭 family

​ Thank you for your question. I have a happy family. My family is warm and harmonious. There are three members in my family: farther, mother and I. My mother is a very warm-hearted person. She often teaches me to help people in need. My father is a kind, upright and hard-working man. I am very optimistic, which is due to my father tell me to learn to adjust my emotions. I love them and thank them very much. That’s all, Thank you for your time.

9.爱好 hobby

​ Thank you for your question. I have a lot of hobbies, such as listening to music, reading books ,dancing and so on. But my favorite are jogging and reading books, which not only broadened my views, but also enrich my life. Jogging can help me keep a good physical condition. I like reading books, which is due to my teacher Mr xx from undergraduate college. His academic and thought infected me and attracted me to the academic road. That’s all, Thank you for your time.
